About Build and Learn

This is a place for everyone who is eager to learn, build and collaborate.

Web Development

In this track you wil build a website of choice with other developers from around the world. You will work as a remote team for 4 weeks.

iOS Development

In this track you will work on a app of choice with a remote team from around the world for 6 weeks. You wil learn how to work in a remote team as a member of a team lead.

Android Development

In this track you will work on a Android App of choice with a remote team for 6 weeks. You can join as a team member of a team leader.
